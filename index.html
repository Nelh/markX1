Stuntmark (ios)

01 How does this work?

StuntMark uses geofencing technology to create virtual boundaries around specific areas. When a user enters or moves within these zones, the app automatically triggers relevant posts, updates, or alerts about the location. This could include information about places to avoid, events, or nearby activities, ensuring users receive real-time, location-specific insights.

02 Product

  • Location-Based Content Discovery: Users receive real-time posts and updates about nearby places, events, and activities based on their current location.
  • Personalized AI Recommendations: The app tailors suggestions and insights to user preferences, highlighting relevant local businesses, events, or points of interest.
  • Geofenced Alerts: StuntMark uses geofencing technology to send proactive notifications when users enter specific areas, keeping them informed about what’s happening around them.
  • Community Engagement: Users can create, share, and comment on posts tied to specific locations, fostering real-world community discussions and interactions.
  • Dynamic Feed Refresh: As users move, the app automatically updates the content feed, providing the latest posts and events relevant to their immediate surroundings.

03 Use cases

  • Discovering local events in real-time.
  • Avoiding unsafe or problematic locations.
  • Promoting small businesses and local gems.
  • Reporting and raising awareness of neighborhood issues.
  • Tracking local trends and movements.
  • Discovering hidden local spots while traveling.
